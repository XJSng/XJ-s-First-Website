Introduction to Flexbox

What is a Flexbox?

Think of flexbox as a more efficient way to layout, align, and distribute space among items in a container, even then their size is unknown or dynamic. Its giving element the autonomy to alther their size to best fill the available space giving to it. For more information on flex box, you can check out the Complete Flexbox Guide.
