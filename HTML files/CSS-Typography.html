Custom Fonts

There are two ways to put custom font on your website

  • Hosted Font Solutions

    • Hosted Font Solutions are plug and play font solutions you can get from Adobe TypeKit (paid) or Google Font (free). They are quite easy to us where you plug and paste into your code.

  • Self Hosted Fonts

    • These are Font files that are on our servers and we have to manage our servers and link to our files. This will be what we will discuss below. But first you will need a font, which you can download from Font Squirrel and you will need 4 different file types of different devices which are the .eot, .svg, .off, .woff. Next we will shift the fonts into our hosting folder in my case it is called "HTML" but yours might be call differently.
